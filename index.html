    About me

    I presently hold the position of Associate Professor with Special Appointment at Hangzhou Dianzi University. I received my Ph.D. degree from Fudan University, earning the honor of Outstanding Graduate in 2023. Prior to that, I earned my bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2018. Between 2018 and 2023, I conducted research in a collaborative doctoral program between Westlake University and Fudan University. My current research topics cover Deep Learning (DL), Computer Vision (CV), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Embodied Intelligence (Robotics), with a focus on integrating CV and NLP within the field of Robotics. I have conducted both basic and applied research, including multi-modal perception and reasoning, visual-language robotic tasks, and Large Language Models (LLMs), etc. My publication portfolio includes over 20 papers in top-tier conferences and journals, such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, AAAI, IJCAI, ACM MM, IROS, ICASSP, ECAI, and TCSVT. My Google Scholar citations exceed 800. I have applied for over 10 invention patents. I also serve as a Program Committee Member (or Reviewer) for top conferences, including CVPR, AAAI, IJCAI, ACM MM, ICML, and ICLR, as well as top journals including TIP (IF=13.7), TCSVT (IF=11.1), Neurocomputing (IF=6.5), etc. In addition, I have been serving as an Editor / Guest Editor for TCSVT (IF=11.1, Special Issue: Large Language Models (LLMs) for Video Understanding) and other SCI journals since 2024.
