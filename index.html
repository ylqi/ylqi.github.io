    About me

    I am a Ph.D. student at Fudan University. I am also with School of Engineering at Westlake University. I earned my bachelor degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2018. My current research topic covers Deep Learning (DL), Computer Vision (CV), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Robotic Navigation. I am focusing on combining the computer vision with natural language processing on the platform of robotics. I have conducted basic and applied researches including video captioning, visual-language robotic navigation, and visual geo-localization, etc. My publication portfolio includes papers from top-tier conferences and journals, including ICCV, AAAI, IJCAI, IROS, ICASSP and TCSVT. I severed as chair of Westlake University ACM Student Chapter in 2021. I also serve as Program Committee Member (PC Member) for AAAI-23 and IJCAI-23.
